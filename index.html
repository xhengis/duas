99 Duas Inspired by the Beautiful Names of Allah

Scroll through the names below to jump directly to each dua.

1

Ya Rahmanيا رحمن

The Most Merciful

From Your Throne You wrote mercy upon Yourself.

You were merciful before there was anyone to receive mercy.

Embrace every part of me with that mercy: my past with its scars, my present with its needs, and my future with its uncertainties.

Let me taste the mercy You keep for those who return.

Reward me with the mercy I have yet to know, and raise me to the highest place in Paradise despite my lows.

Cover me from storms I do not see coming, mend me before I break, and spare me from trials of every kind.

Let Your mercy reach me in ways I will only understand when I finally meet You.

2

Ya Hayyيا حي

The Ever-Living

The One my heart was created to seek.

Pull me away from every false worship, from anything that divides my devotion, until nothing fills my longing but You.

Unify my pursuits into a single direction, and when the world pulls me apart, center me in Your oneness.

3

Ya Ahadيا أحد

The One

Open my heart to see that nothing compares to You, so that I am never overly impressed by anyone but You.

Let me love Your names and attributes in a way that frees me from all others but You.

4

Ya Wahidيا واحد

The Unique

When I stand alone at the close of the night, let my solitude remind me of Your singular majesty.

Make my heart distinct by its devotion, and make me one of Your rarest servants.

5

Ya Hadiيا هادي

The Guide

Guide me when my heart forgets the way.

Pull me back from every turn that leads me astray.

Let Your guidance feel closer than the doubts in my chest.

6

Ya Rashidيا رشيد

The Guide to the Right Path

Teach me to see truth instinctively.

Make faith beloved to me, make righteousness feel natural, and let sin feel heavy on my chest and hated by my every breath.

Inspire me with wisdom that protects me even from myself.

7

Ya Nurيا نور

The Light

Light every darkness I carried or carry within.

Let Your light pour into my heart and onto all my limbs until You make me a light, and everything I see reminds me of You.

8

Ya Mubinيا مبين

The Manifest One

Make Your signs clear in what surrounds me and what is within me.

Reveal the lessons hidden in every trial.

Let me never lose sight of Your signs again.

9

Ya Waliيا ولي

The Protecting Friend

Be my closest friend when the world drifts away.

Guard me with a grip that never slips.

Keep me firm on the ground of Your goodness.

Make my faith steady when my heart trembles.

Let me love what brings me to Your stability, and make me patient with what keeps me on its shore.

10

Ya Rafiqيا رفيق

The Gentle Companion

Be gentle with my soul as You unfold Your plan.

Soften the path without removing its purpose.

Make me gentle with others as You have been gentle with me, and when I am lonely, fill that space with Your company.

11

Ya Namiيا نامي

The One Who Causes Growth

Raise me the way You raise every seed from the soil.

Shape my growth with all You continue to give.

Let every season You place me in become a lesson in Your care.

12

Ya Mawla

The Protector and Master

Be my protector when I have no one else to stand up for me.

Take charge of my affairs when I lose control of them.

Hold me near when fear surrounds me, and make Your guardianship always enough for me.

13

Ya Nasirيا نصير

The Helper

Help me in every battle I cannot see.

Grant me triumph without arrogance, and victory only in a way that brings me closer to You.

14

Ya Azizيا عزيز

The Almighty

Teach my heart that true honor is to serve only You.

Let my dignity be in humility, my leadership in obedience, and my freedom in surrender.

15

Ya Malikيا ملك

The King

You are the King of everything I know and hold.

Remind me that nothing I hold is truly mine and that every rise and fall belongs to Your decree.

Let my gratitude expand with every breath You lend to me.

16

Ya Malik Yawm ad-Din

Master of the Day of Judgment

Owner of the day when debts are paid.

Teach me to settle my dues before I meet You.

Keep me honest with what I owe and hopeful in what You forgive.

17

Ya Malik al-Mulkيا مالك الملك

The King of Kings

The King whose command no one can escape.

Let Your sovereignty humble my pride.

Make me content under Your rule, and protect me from a rebellious heart that would cause me to fall out of Your favor.

18

Ya Dhu’l-Jalalيا ذو الجلال

The Possessor of Majesty

Fill my heart with reverence that draws me near, not fear that drives me away.

Let my awe of You refine me until I stand before You small but never disgraced.

19

Ya Khaliqيا خالق

The Creator

You brought me out of nothingness into being.

Remind me that my existence itself is a mercy, that every breath, every cell, and every chance is a deliberate act of Your creation.

20

Ya Bariيا بارئ

The Evolver

The One who perfects what He produces.

Repair what I have broken within myself.

Make me whole again with the same precision by which You fashioned all of Your creation.

21

Ya Musawwirيا مصور

The Fashioner

You shaped me in the unseen, gave me form, face, and story.

Beautify my character as You have beautified my features.

Let what You see within me be more pleasing than what others see outside of me.

22

Ya Fatirيا فاطر

The Originator

Keep creating new openings when I run out of ways.

Create for me new beginnings when I rush to my end, and new realities when I feel doomed by my sins.

Remake me with patience when I reach my limits, faith when I am afraid, and hope when I cannot imagine a way forward.

23

Ya Samadيا صمد

The Eternal Refuge

The One who needs nothing while everything needs You.

Make me whole by depending only on You.

Fill the hollowness inside of me until no desire within me competes with Your glory.

24

Ya Haqqيا حق

The Truth

Make Your truth the anchor of my heart and my guiding path to Your light.

Let every lie I chase collapse before Your reality, and every truth I seek lead me to Your next promise.

Keep me grounded in what endures, and turn my eyes away from what deceives.

25

Ya Wasiيا واسع

The All-Encompassing

Expand my heart to hold more gratitude, my patience to endure with more grace, and my vision to see beyond my limits.

26

Ya Ghafurيا غفور

The All-Forgiving

Forgive me for every single sin I have ever put forth.

Cover me in this life and the next, and do not let shame keep me from returning to You.

27

Ya Ghaffarيا غفار

The Oft-Forgiving

For every time I fall and rise again, meet me with another chance.

Let my heart grow softer with every repentance, and let my view of the door of Your mercy only grow wider, for You have never tired of forgiving me despite my constant sins.

Forgive me for what I know and what I do not, what I have done and what I still may do, and turn the stains of my past into light on my scrolls.

Reach me with Your forgiveness even in the holes I have dug for myself.

28

Ya Tawwabيا تواب

The Accepter of Repentance

Turn toward me so that I may turn back to You, and accept me every time I return.

Make repentance my constant companion and my joy in finding You again.

29

Ya Wadudيا ودود

The Most Loving

Love me even after I falter, and let Your love reach me through every mercy I overlook.

Teach me to love You more than what You give, and to find sweetness in every test that draws me closer to Your love.

Announce Your love for me in the heavens until I hear that announcement myself in Paradise.

30

Ya Muhsinيا محسن

The Bestower of Good

You perfect everything You touch.

Make me one of those who do good because You do good, then let my deeds of excellence toward others be a reflection of Your unmatched perfection.

31

Ya Shakurيا شكور

The Most Appreciative

For every small effort I make, reward me beyond measure.

Let me never grow tired of thanking You, and never forget that gratitude itself is a gift from You.

Accept from me what is broken, and multiply it until it becomes whole.

32

Ya Samiيا سميع

The All-Hearing

You hear every sound before it is spoken.

Inspire in me the words that are pleasing to You.

Make sincere the dua that I utter between breaths.

Let me never doubt that You are listening, and never tire of my calling.

33

Ya Qaribيا قريب

The Near One

You are nearer than every thought I hide.

Let me feel Your nearness in the stillness of my prayer.

Let the clarity and comfort of Your closeness cut through all the noise outside, and forgive me for what should have never made it inside.

34

Ya Mujibيا مجيب

The Responder

Answer not only what I ask, but what I mean.

Give me what I would have prayed for if I knew what was best for me.

When I raise my hands to You, fill them only with what draws me closer to You.

35

Ya Mu’min

The Giver of Faith

Grant me faith that no fear can shake.

Secure my heart in Your promise, and guard my soul from every whisper that breeds doubt.

36

Ya Muhayminيا مهيمن

The Overseer

The Watchful One who never sleeps.

Watch over me in the moments that no one else can, and be my shield from even what I see but cannot change.

Preserve my faith through trial and time.

Guard my loved ones when they are far away from me.

Keep safe the blessings I forgot to thank You for, and protect me, for without You I cannot protect myself.

37

Ya Wakilيا وكيل

The Disposer of Affairs

I entrust to You my every matter, what I seemingly can handle and that which is obviously beyond my control.

Take charge of what confuses me and carry what I cannot hold.

38

Ya Basirيا بصير

The All-Seeing

You see what no one else sees, every tear I hide, every effort unnoticed.

Let me find comfort in being seen by You when I feel invisible to the rest of the world.

39

Ya Shahidيا شهيد

The Witness

Be my witness when my intentions are pure and when they falter.

Let my fear of You set them aright.

Let me live conscious that You are always truly watching, and die as one who lived truthfully under Your sight.

40

Ya Raqibيا رقيب

The Watchful

Watch over my heart when it drifts and over my thoughts before they turn to deeds.

Make me mindful of You in secret and in public until awareness of You becomes my protection.

41

Ya Sattarيا ستار

The Veiler of Faults

Cover my faults with the same grace You always have.

Hide my flaws even as You heal them.

Let my repentance be quiet, my shame sincere, and my forgiveness made complete beneath Your protective veil.

42

Ya Subhanيا سبحان

The Perfect One

How perfect You are beyond everything I can imagine.

When the world feels loud and heavy, remind me that You are untouched by its chaos.

Let my glorification and praise of You rise above the darkness.

43

Ya Quddusيا قدوس

The Holy One

Purify me inside and out.

Cleanse my worship of pride, my heart of resentment, and my soul of anything that clouds its way back to You.

44

Ya Salamيا سلام

The Source of Peace

Send peace into every storm within my chest.

Let Your calm settle where fear once lived.

Make me a source of peace for others until I meet You in the Home of Peace.

45

Ya Ghaniيا غني

The Self-Sufficient

You are free of all need while I am always in need of You.

Let me never beg from those as poor as me.

Make me rich through contentment, and fill my heart with gratitude instead of greed.

46

Ya Hamidيا حميد

The Praiseworthy

You are praised whether or not I remember to say it.

Let my praise echo in hardship as it does in ease, and let every breath I take be a quiet thank You.

47

Ya Fattahيا فتاح

The Opener

Open doors I cannot find and hearts that I cannot reach.

Open paths to good where I only see walls, and open my chest to trust whatever You have decreed.

48

Ya Qabid / Ya Basitيا قابض / يا باسط

The Withholder / The Expander

When You withhold, let me not lose hope, and when You expand, let me not lose humility.

Teach me to find You in both the squeeze and the release.

49

Ya Muqaddim / Ya Mu’akhkhir

The Advancer / The Delayer

When You bring me forward, make me worthy of the moment, and when You hold me back, make me patient with the waiting.

Advance me only when the step will please You, and delay me only when the pause will protect me.

50

Ya ‘Alim

The All-Knowing

You know what I hide and what I show, what I have done and what I will do.

Let what You know of me be better than what people know of me, and forgive me for what only You know.

51

Ya Hakimيا حكيم

The Wise

Your wisdom surrounds what I cannot understand.

When I question the purpose, remind me of Your perfection.

Grant me the patience to trust Your timing and the insight to see the beauty unfolding in Your decree.

52

Ya Latifيا لطيف

The Subtle One

Work gently through the details of my life.

Send kindnesses that I may not notice until I look back but can still feel at every moment.

Let Your subtle care reach the corners of my heart that no words or people could ever touch.

53

Ya Khabirيا خبير

The All-Aware

You are fully aware of what is within me, my fears, my flaws, and my hidden intentions.

Purify what You see inside of me until it is pleasing to You.

54

Ya Qahharيا قهار

The Subduer

Subdue every tyrant who raises a hand against the innocent.

Break the arrogance of those who spill the blood of Your beloved servants.

Subdue also the arrogance within me so that I never resemble the oppressor in any form.

55

Ya Jabbarيا جبار

The Restorer / The Compeller

Mend what their hands have broken, rebuild the lands they have shattered, and heal the hearts that tremble for You.

Set right the fractures in our ummah, and make every wound a doorway back to You.

56

Ya Mutakabbirيا متكبر

The Majestic / The Supreme

You are too glorious for injustice to prevail, too mighty for oppression to last.

Show the tyrants that no throne stands forever, and honor the humble who still call Your name even beneath the rubble.

57

Ya Qadirيا قادر

The Able / The Powerful

Nothing is beyond Your power.

As You part the seas and revive the dead, lift our people from beneath the weight of this world.

Show us that no injustice is too deep for You to overturn.

58

Ya Muqtadirيا مقتدر

The All-Powerful

Your power is perfect.

Let those who boast of might see how small they really are.

Crush the arrogance that claims control over Your creation, and remind us that even the most helpless among us are upheld by You alone.

59

Ya Muqsitيا مقسط

The Equitable

O You whose balance never tips unfairly.

When the world feels rigged for cruelty, remind us that You are merely delaying the cruel and never unjust.

Make us witnesses to Your justice in this life and part of its fulfillment in the next.

60

Ya Hakamيا حكم

The Judge

You are the Judge when the courts of men fail.

When truth is buried beneath lies, let Your verdict rise clear and unstoppable.

61

Ya Hasibيا حسيب

The Accounter

You are enough when proof is hidden and power is abused.

Account for every tear that fell unseen and every cruelty that was silenced by fear.

62

Ya Razzaqيا رزاق

The Provider

You provide for every creature in ways we cannot see.

Feed my heart before my hands, and let my trust in Your sustenance outweigh my fear of the future.

63

Ya Karimيا كريم

The Most Generous

You give beyond what I deserve.

Let Your generosity flow through me as a reflection of how it flows from You.

64

Ya Akramيا أكرم

The Most Bountiful

Honor me with contentment in every decree.

Make my share of this world enough and my portion of the next abundant.

65

Ya Qawiيا قوي

The Strong

Strengthen me where I am weak and make me firm where I bend too easily.

Lift me so that I can lift others without ever believing I lifted myself.

66

Ya Matinيا متين

The Firm One

Make me steady when everything else shakes.

Let my faith be anchored deep enough so that no loss can uproot it.

67

Ya Adlيا عدل

The Utterly Just

When injustice clouds the world around me, remind me that Your justice never sleeps.

Let my heart rest in the certainty that every wrong will be answered and every oppressed soul restored.

Make me among those who seek fairness in my actions while trusting the perfection of Your judgment.

68

Ya Mu'izzيا معز

The Bestower of Honor

Honor me not with status that fades but with a heart that remembers You.

Let my worth never depend on the praise of people or the recognition of this world.

Raise me through sincerity so that my honor is found only in serving You.

69

Ya Mudhillيا مذل

The One Who Humiliates

Protect me from the humiliation that comes from arrogance and distance from You.

If pride begins to rise in my heart, humble me before it blinds me.

Let humility be my protection and obedience my dignity.

70

Ya Khafidيا خافض

The One Who Lowers

Lower within me every arrogance that distances me from Your mercy.

Bring down the pride that clouds my judgment and weakens my sincerity.

Let every lowering of my ego become a step toward a purer heart.

71

Ya Rafi'يا رافع

The One Who Raises

Raise my rank through sincerity rather than recognition.

Elevate my soul through acts done quietly for Your sake.

Lift my heart above the distractions of this world so that my focus remains on You.

72

Ya Muqitيا مقيت

The Sustainer

Sustain me not only with provision for my body but nourishment for my soul.

Feed my heart with remembrance and strengthen my spirit with faith.

Let every blessing remind me that my life is sustained only by You.

73

Ya Hafizيا حفيظ

The Preserver

Preserve my faith when trials surround me and doubts appear.

Protect my heart from influences that weaken my trust in You.

Guard my path so that every step I take moves me closer to Your pleasure.

74

Ya Jalilيا جليل

The Majestic

Fill my heart with awe of Your greatness so that nothing in this world overwhelms me again.

Let the majesty of Your presence quiet every fear within me.

Remind me that the power of creation is small before Your majesty.

75

Ya Majidيا ماجد

The Glorious

Let my heart recognize Your glory in every creation around me.

Remind me that every beauty I witness is only a reflection of Your perfection.

Fill my soul with gratitude whenever I see the signs of Your greatness.

76

Ya Majeedيا مجيد

The Most Noble

Grant me a share of nobility through character and sincerity.

Let kindness, patience, and truth shape the way I live among others.

Make my actions reflect the honor that comes from serving You.

77

Ya Waliyyيا ولي

The Protecting Guardian

Be my guardian in every moment of weakness.

Stand beside me when I feel alone and uncertain.

Guide me with a protection that keeps my heart firm in faith.

78

Ya Muqsitيا مقسط

The Equitable

When the world appears unfair and unbalanced, remind me that Your scales never tilt unjustly.

Let my heart remain patient until Your wisdom becomes clear.

Make me among those who trust Your justice even before they witness it.

79

Ya Jamiيا جامع

The Gatherer

Gather my scattered thoughts and restless worries.

Bring together the broken pieces of my hopes and intentions.

Unite my heart around a single purpose that leads me back to You.

80

Ya Mughniيا مغني

The Enricher

Enrich my heart with contentment so that I never feel poor while remembering You.

Let the wealth of my soul outweigh the riches of this world.

Make gratitude my greatest treasure and closeness to You my greatest wealth.

81

Ya Maniيا مانع

The Withholder

If something is withheld from me, let me recognize it as Your protection.

Guard me from blessings that would harm my faith or mislead my heart.

Let every prevention from You become a mercy I learn to trust.

82

Ya Darrيا ضار

The One Who Allows Harm

When hardship reaches me, let it bring me closer to You rather than further away.

Turn every pain into purification and every trial into growth.

Let difficulty strengthen my patience and deepen my reliance upon You.

83

Ya Nafiيا نافع

The Bestower of Benefit

Grant me benefits that endure beyond this life.

Let the good I receive inspire goodness toward others.

Make every blessing a bridge that leads me closer to Your pleasure.

84

Ya Badiيا بديع

The Originator

Create for me new beginnings when hope fades within my heart.

Show me possibilities I never imagined and paths I never considered.

Remind me that Your creativity knows no limits and Your mercy always renews.

85

Ya Baqiيا باقي

The Everlasting

Remind me that everything in this world will fade except You.

Anchor my heart to what remains when all else disappears.

Let my love for what is eternal outweigh my attachment to what is temporary.

86

Ya Warithيا وارث

The Inheritor

When all things pass away and return to You, let my only inheritance be Your mercy.

Make my legacy one of faith and goodness that remains after I am gone.

Allow the traces I leave behind to lead others closer to remembering You.

87

Ya Rashidيا رشيد

The Guide to the Right Path

Guide me toward wisdom in every decision I make.

Let patience and reflection shape my understanding of life.

Help me grow into a person whose actions reflect Your guidance.

88

Ya Saburيا صبور

The Most Patient

Teach me patience that reflects the mercy You show toward me.

When delays frustrate me, remind me of the wisdom in Your timing.

Let endurance become a strength that deepens my trust in You.

89

Ya Qadirيا قادر

The All-Powerful

Remind me that nothing lies beyond Your power.

When I feel trapped by circumstances, show me that Your decree can change everything.

Let my hope remain firm in the limitless strength of Your will.

90

Ya Muqtadirيا مقتدر

The Determiner

You control what others believe they control.

Show me that every authority in this world exists only through Your permission.

Let my trust rest in Your power rather than the power of people.

91

Ya Qawiيا قوي

The Most Strong

Strengthen me when my resolve weakens and my patience fades.

Grant me strength that comes from reliance upon You.

Let my perseverance become a reflection of Your strength supporting me.

92

Ya Matinيا متين

The Firm

Make my faith steady when everything around me feels uncertain.

Anchor my heart so deeply in trust that no storm can uproot it.

Let my reliance on You become the foundation of my strength.

93

Ya Hakimيا حكيم

The Most Wise

When I struggle to understand what happens around me, remind me of Your wisdom.

Let me trust that every decree carries a purpose beyond what I can see.

Grant me patience until Your wisdom becomes clear.

94

Ya Latifيا لطيف

The Most Subtle

Work gently through the details of my life.

Send kindness I may not notice until I look back.

Let Your subtle care reach the corners of my heart that no one else can reach.

95

Ya Khabirيا خبير

The All-Aware

You know what lies within my heart before I speak it.

Purify my intentions and guide my hidden thoughts toward goodness.

Let what You know of me be better than what people see.

96

Ya Samiيا سميع

The All-Hearing

You hear every whisper before it reaches my lips.

Accept the prayers I struggle to express.

Let my voice always rise toward You with sincerity.

97

Ya Basirيا بصير

The All-Seeing

You see what no one else notices.

Every effort, every tear, every hidden struggle is known to You.

Let the comfort of being seen by You strengthen my heart.

98

Ya Qaribيا قريب

The Near

You are nearer to me than I realize.

Let me feel Your closeness in the stillness of my prayers.

Bring peace to my heart through the certainty that You are always near.

99

Ya Allahيا الله

The Greatest Name

You are the One whose name contains every perfection.

Let every prayer, every hope, and every breath lead me back to You.

Accept my return, forgive my shortcomings, and allow my final words to be remembrance of You.
